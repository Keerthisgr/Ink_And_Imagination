The Power of Perspective!!

As we grow up, we learn to be resilient. We adapt, adjust and handle. But how do we deal with the pain that remains!?

The key is to change our perspective. We can see those experiences as lessons that help us grow and improve. We can be brave, face our fears, and overcome the pain.

The unseen wounds of job scams

Spoorthi, 23 years old, left her hometown to find a job in the silicon city, driven by a singular focus: supporting her ailing mother and younger brother. Her family's future depended on her success. As she navigated unfamiliar streets, her heart longed for the warmth of home. Every rejection letter and unfulfilled promise weighed heavily on her shoulders.

Desperate and trusting, Spoorthi received a fake phone call promising a dream job. She paid ₹5,000 as a “processing fee.” However, the job offer was suddenly cancelled, revealing the harsh truth: she had fallen victim to a job scam.

The scam’s impact on Spoorthi’s mental health was suffering: Sleepless nights Loss of craving Constant worry As an innocent victim of fraud, Spoorthi faced: Inner conflicts Financial ruin Loss of trust Spoorthi’s story is not unique. Countless women, driven by responsibility and desperation, fall victim to scams, their innocence and trust exploited, leaving lasting emotional marks.
